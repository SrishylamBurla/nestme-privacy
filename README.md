NestMe ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy.

* Name, email address, and phone number
* Property preferences and activity

We use your data to:

* Provide property listings and services

* Send notifications and updates

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

* Service providers (for app functionality)
* Legal authorities if required

## Notifications

We may send push notifications related to properties, updates, and activity.

## Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data.
